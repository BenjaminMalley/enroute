On your way?

En route makes it easy to share your location and destination with friends and family.

How it works

Once you're signed in with Twitter, begin a new trip by specifying a destination in the destination bar.
En route sends out a tweet from your account containing a URL for your followers to track your progress towards the destination.
When you get to your destination, En route sends out another tweet letting your followers know you've arrived and stops tracking your location.
